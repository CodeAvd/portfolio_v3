# Concept Brief

Design a premium personal portfolio for Grigorii with an `editorial cinema` direction.

The site must do two jobs at once:

1. Act as a memorable personal showcase with a strong first impression.
2. Act as a trust document for recruiters and hiring managers.

Core rules:

- Home is a trailer plus recruiter summary.
- Resume is a premium hiring dossier, not a landing page.
- Cases are proof appendix pages with disciplined narrative structure.
- Motion is allowed, but clarity must never be sacrificed for spectacle.
- The profile should read support-first, with systems leverage and builder proof as supporting evidence.

The aesthetic should feel premium, sharp, and intentional:

- editorial serif headlines
- restrained sans-serif body
- mono labels and timecode-like metadata
- film-still framing
- subtle grain and frame lines
- no glassmorphism overload
- no generic SaaS dashboard chrome
- no crypto terminal cosplay

Primary CTA hierarchy:

1. Open resume
2. Email / contact
3. Read flagship case
