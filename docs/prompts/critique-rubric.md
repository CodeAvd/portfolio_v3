# Critique Rubric

Score the page from 0 to 100 using these weighted categories:

- Visual distinction: 25
- Profile clarity: 25
- Proof of competence: 20
- Case narrative: 15
- CTA persuasiveness: 15

The review must not stop at "beautiful" or "ugly".

The reviewer must answer with concrete evidence:

1. What is visible above the fold?
2. Is the target profile obvious within 8 seconds?
3. Which proof signals are visible before the second scroll?
4. Does the flagship case actually strengthen trust?
5. Is the dominant CTA clear without searching?
6. Which motion moments help the message?
7. Which motion moments interfere with reading or scanning?
8. What is memorable after one pass?
9. What is still vague or underspecified about the profile?
10. What exact change would most improve recruiter conversion?

Reviewers must cite specific sections or elements, not general taste.
