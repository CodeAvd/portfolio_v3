# Research, Audit, And Competitor Matrix

## Six-layer audit of the previous identity

| Layer | Previous read | Keep | Replace |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Layout | editorial split hero with a large portrait | case-study flow and information density | portrait-led hierarchy |
| Typography | warm sans + mono, premium but soft | mono labels for proof and metadata | softer headline rhythm |
| Color | warm paper, teal/coral, luxe gradients | paper-toned light mode base | coral/teal ambience and glow styling |
| Components | glass cards, blurred pills, soft hover lift | modular cards and clear CTA grouping | glassmorphism and high-polish chrome |
| Motion | cinematic fades and ambient drift | measured reveal timing | floating orb atmosphere |
| Character | premium personal portfolio | disciplined writing and recruiter-safe clarity | “luxury personal site” read |

## Competitor buckets

| Bucket | Recognizable pattern | Why it fails for this portfolio | Deliberate counter-move |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Generic software-engineer portfolios | SaaS cards, gradients, product-marketing polish | reads too broad and understates support evidence | use dossier cards and evidence rails |
| Notion/Framer case-study sites | beige editorial minimalism and personal-photo dominance | feels tasteful but not operator-grade | shift the hero to proof, labels, and operating lanes |
| Support/resume landing pages | résumé-first utility with little visual memory | credible but forgettable | add a distinct mark system and evidence-board language |

## Moodboard direction

The final direction mixes:

- Swiss systems layout for alignment, rule lines, and modular spacing
- editorial typography for authority and memorability
- developer-tool component language for controls, chips, and evidence grouping
- field-manual color logic for paper, graphite, signal sage, oxide, and amber

## Clichés banned in review

- SaaS dashboard chrome as the dominant visual language
- beige luxury styling that puts atmosphere ahead of proof
- crypto/dev terminal cosplay or neon “operator” aesthetics
